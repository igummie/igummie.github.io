AverageMusic Bot

Filters!

AverageMusic allows you to use filters to enhance your music experience ~ This bot is built off of Focabot by TheBITLINK

For example:

!play Gary come home | speed=1.5

Will play Gary come home at 1.5x speed!

You can chain multiple filters as well:

!play triple-1 nuclear winter | speed=0.75 reverse lowpass=1000

Average bot can handle many filters at once!


You can also change filters while the song plays:

!| earrape volume=20 bass=20

You can only do this if you're a DJ/Bot Commander or if the current song was requested by you.

Available Filters!

| speed

Changes the speed of the song. (both pitch and tempo).
Speed must be in the 0.1x~10x range.

| tempo

Changes the speed (tempo) of the song without altering the pitch.
Speed must be in the 0.5x~2x range.

| reverse

Reverses the song.

| echo

An exagerated reverb effect.

| lowpass

Attenuates frequencies higher than the cutoff frequency.

| highpass

Attenuates frequencies lower than the cutoff frequency.

| bass

Bass Boost Filter. Use 20 for maximum boost

| treble

Like | bass, but boosts the higher frequencies.

| flanger

Flanger Effect. (trippy af if you use stereo headphones)

| volume

Amplifies the song. (Also produces ear rape if used with high values)
Volume must be in the -40dB~40dB range.

| karaoke

Attempts to remove all chorus in the song

| chorus

Makes the audio sound like a chorus.

| phaser

alien shit

| tremolo

The volume goes constantly up and down.

| loop

Loops a section of the audio.

You must use this format:

loop=[start]-[end]-[number of loops]

| nofx

dumb thing no one ever uses that prevents changest to current filters
